Love and Hate

I have a real love/hate relationship with the Web.

I love that it can connect anyone anywhere at anytime, but I hate that it can connect people around the wrong things.

I love how it is malleable and nothing is ever fixed, but I hate having to change everything all the time because someone didn't think about what to do before we did it.

I love that the code is easy to learn and use, but I hate that the same ease of use hurts my ability to make money building things on the Web.

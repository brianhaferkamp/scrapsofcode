Website Critique

There is a lot to web development and design. Some of the best tutorial series I've come across are the ones that take you through the entire process of designing and developing a website. For a company that already has a website this starts with a critique of the current site. Today I'm sharing my critique process with you. I look at three main areas: Design, Functionality, and Responsiveness. To me, these are the three top level keys to modern design and development. I'll go through the site and point out what is working and what is not.

My plan is that this will kick off a series of videos that show the entire redesign process from start to finish. Keep tuned for those.

No Comment

This blog is sort of an experiment in ideas that I have. One of those ideas was to use Twitter cards as a commenting system. I knew that this was limited (if not doomed) from the beginning for this obvious reason: Not everyone is on Twitter. But I thought if I only talked about it on Twitter that I would get mostly people with Twitter accounts. That might have happened, but there have been months with no comments, so I'm pulling the plug on comments altogether. I realize that comments have largely been on their way out over the years, so I'm really just joining the herd.

On another house cleaning note, if you'd like to catch up on previous posts, check out the archive page.
